About Our Department

Our Mission

To develop Computer Professionals by imparting computer engineering knowledge with professional ethics. To provide service to the communities to which we belong at local and national levels, combined with a deep awareness of our ethical responsibilities to our profession and to society.

Our Vision

To develop engineers well versed with Critical Theory and Practicals (problem solving ability) and sensitive to National and Global challenges from an Inter-disciplinary perspective. To create industry-ready, socially and ethically strong professionals.

Department Overview

The Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML) Department at PCET’s Pimpri Chinchwad University aims to nurture innovation and technical excellence. Our department bridges academic learning and real-world applications through advanced AI-driven research, projects, and collaborations with industry experts.

With a focus on interdisciplinary learning, we empower students to build intelligent systems that transform the world — from automation and data science to responsible AI innovation.
